The through-line

There is a quiet math beneath every account I manage — weighted signals, health scores, the small arithmetic of trust. For two years it has kept a ₹384 Cr portfolio at Airtel from churning. Then I began turning that same thinking into software.

I'm an Enterprise Account Manager at Bharti Airtel, owning one of its largest strategic B2B portfolios — ICICI Bank, Asian Paints, Credit Suisse, Godrej — across cloud, connectivity and security. I cut churn 46% with a customer health-scoring model, recovered ₹60L+ in contract value, and put LLM-based AI agents into a live enterprise environment, eliminating 80% of recurring incidents. Away from the desk, I take the models I trust and ship them as public products: a marketing-intelligence platform, a supply-chain digital twin, a firewall vulnerability scanner, and a design-system generator — each built end to end, each live for anyone to use. MBA from XIMB; certified in Zero Trust, Zscaler, Product Management and Lean Six Sigma.
